Cloud Services Overview

Cloud services are internet-based offerings that provide scalable and flexible resources for computing, storage, and applications, eliminating the need for physical hardware and reducing IT costs.

They are categorized into Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) like AWS and Microsoft Azure, Platform as a Service (PaaS) such as Google App Engine and Microsoft Azure App Services, and Software as a Service (SaaS) like Microsoft Office 365 and Salesforce.

Benefits include scalability, cost efficiency, flexibility, reliability, and enhanced security. These advantages allow businesses to focus on core activities while utilizing advanced technology managed by third-partyâ€¯providers.
